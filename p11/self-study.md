## B. GRAMMAR SELF-STUDY

### B1. Grammar Summary: Future Forms & Adjectives/Adverbs

#### Future Form 1: WILL + base verb

**Form:**
- Positive: Subject + will + base verb
- Negative: Subject + will not (won't) + base verb
- Question: Will + subject + base verb?

**Use:**
1. **Predictions** (based on opinion): "I think the app will be successful."
2. **Spontaneous decisions**: "I'll add that feature right now."
3. **Promises**: "We'll fix the bug by Friday."

**App Development Examples:**
- "The app will revolutionize mobile fitness."
- "Users will love the new interface."

---

#### Future Form 2: GOING TO + base verb

**Form:**
- Positive: Subject + am/is/are + going to + base verb

**Use:**
1. **Plans and intentions**: "We're going to develop a fitness app."
2. **Predictions based on evidence**: "Look at the downloads ‚Äî the app is going to be huge!"

**App Development Examples:**
- "We're going to target young professionals."
- "They're going to use Flutter for development."

---

#### Future Form 3: Present Continuous for Future

**Use:** Fixed arrangements with specific time/place
- "I'm meeting with investors tomorrow at 10 AM."
- "We're launching the beta next Friday."

---

#### Future Form 4: Present Simple for Future

**Use:** Timetables and schedules
- "The app store review takes 48 hours."
- "Our sprint starts on Monday."

---

#### Adjectives & Adverbs

**Adjectives** describe nouns:
- "a **fast** app" / "The app is **intuitive**."
- Comparative: fast ‚Üí faster / efficient ‚Üí more efficient
- Superlative: fast ‚Üí fastest / efficient ‚Üí most efficient

**Adverbs** describe verbs, adjectives, or other adverbs:
- "The app loads **quickly**." / "**extremely** responsive design"
- Comparative: quickly ‚Üí more quickly
- Superlative: quickly ‚Üí most quickly

**Irregular forms:**
- good/well ‚Üí better ‚Üí best
- bad/badly ‚Üí worse ‚Üí worst

**Section 2: Target Audience (2-3 sentences)**

**Section 3: Key Features (3-5 bullet points)**

#### Model Answer

> **FitBuddy: Your AI-Powered Fitness Companion**
>
> Many Indonesian university students struggle to maintain a consistent exercise routine due to busy schedules and limited gym access. FitBuddy is going to solve this by providing personalized, equipment-free workout plans that adapt to each user's schedule and fitness level.
>
> We're going to target university students aged 18-25 who want to stay fit but have limited time and resources. The app will be more accessible than existing fitness apps because all workouts require no equipment.
>
> Key features include: (1) an AI-powered workout generator that creates personalized routines, (2) push notifications with smart scheduling based on class timetables, (3) a social feature where users can challenge friends, (4) progress tracking with data visualization, and (5) integration with health APIs.
>
> We're developing the app using React Native because it is more cost-effective than native development and allows us to deploy on both iOS and Android simultaneously. This is the fastest approach for an MVP launch.
>
> We're launching the beta version next semester with 200 student testers. By Q3, we will have a full commercial release. The health and fitness app market is the fastest-growing category in Southeast Asia, and FitBuddy will be the most student-focused app available.
