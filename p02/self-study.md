## B. GRAMMAR SELF-STUDY

### B1. Grammar Summary: Countable & Uncountable Nouns

#### Countable Nouns

**Definition:** Nouns that can be counted individually (one, two, three...)

**Examples in IT:**
- cores, threads, slots, ports, drives, fans, connectors, cables

**Quantifiers:**
- **many** - "How many cores does the CPU have?"
- **few** - "There are few USB ports on this laptop."
- **fewer** - "This model has fewer RAM slots than the other."
- **several** - "The motherboard has several expansion slots."
- **a number of** - "A number of fans are installed for cooling."

---

#### Uncountable Nouns

**Definition:** Nouns that cannot be counted individually (no plural form)

**Examples in IT:**
- memory, storage, power, bandwidth, heat, performance, speed, electricity

**Quantifiers:**
- **much** - "How much memory does the system have?"
- **little** - "There's little storage space left."
- **less** - "This laptop uses less power than the desktop."
- **a large amount of** - "The server requires a large amount of bandwidth."

---

#### Common Mistakes to Avoid

| ‚ùå WRONG | ‚úÖ CORRECT | Reason |
|----------|-----------|--------|
| many memory | much memory | Memory is uncountable |
| less cores | fewer cores | Cores is countable |
| much fans | many fans | Fans is countable |
| fewer bandwidth | less bandwidth | Bandwidth is uncountable |

### B2. Grammar Summary: Comparatives & Superlatives

#### Formation Rules

| Adjective Type | Comparative | Superlative | Example |
|----------------|-------------|-------------|---------|
| One syllable | + -er | + -est | fast ‚Üí faster ‚Üí the fastest |
| Ends in -e | + -r | + -st | large ‚Üí larger ‚Üí the largest |
| CVC pattern | double + -er | double + -est | big ‚Üí bigger ‚Üí the biggest |
| Two+ syllables | more + adj | the most + adj | powerful ‚Üí more powerful ‚Üí the most powerful |
| Ends in -y | -y ‚Üí -ier | -y ‚Üí -iest | easy ‚Üí easier ‚Üí the easiest |

#### Irregular Forms

| Adjective | Comparative | Superlative |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| good | better | the best |
| bad | worse | the worst |
| little (amount) | less | the least |
| much/many | more | the most |
| far | farther/further | the farthest/furthest |

#### Advanced Comparison Structures

| Structure | Example |
|-----------|---------|
| twice as + adj + as | "The SSD is twice as fast as the HDD." |
| three times more + noun + than | "This PC has three times more storage than that one." |
| significantly/considerably + comparative | "NVMe is significantly faster than SATA." |
| not as + adj + as | "The laptop is not as powerful as the desktop." |
| the + comparative, the + comparative | "The faster the CPU, the better the performance." |
