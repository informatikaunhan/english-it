## B. GRAMMAR SELF-STUDY

### B1. Grammar Summary: Advanced Passive Voice & Conditionals Type 2 & 3

#### Advanced Passive Voice

**Purpose:** Focus on the process or result, not who does the action. Common in technical and academic writing.

**Form:** Subject + be (conjugated) + past participle

| Tense/Type | Structure | AI Example |
|------------|-----------|------------|
| **Present Simple Passive** | is/are + past participle | Data is collected from sensors. |
| **Present Continuous Passive** | is/are being + past participle | The algorithm is being tested right now. |
| **Present Perfect Passive** | has/have been + past participle | The model has been trained for three weeks. |
| **Modal Passive** | can/could/should/must + be + past participle | AI can be used to detect fraud. |
| **Passive Reporting** | It is + past participle + that... | It is believed that AI will transform healthcare. |

**When to Use Passive Voice:**
1. When the doer is unknown or unimportant: "The data was collected."
2. When focusing on the process: "Neural networks are trained on millions of images."
3. In formal/technical writing: "The algorithm is optimized for speed."
4. When the doer is obvious: "The system has been updated."

**IT Examples:**
- "Predictions are made based on historical patterns."
- "The model is being validated on real-world data."
- "Errors have been detected in the training dataset."
- "AI systems must be regulated to prevent misuse."

---

#### Conditional Type 2 (Hypothetical Present/Future)

**Form:** If + past simple, would/could/might + base verb

**Use:** Imaginary or unlikely situations in present/future

**Special Note:** Use "were" (not "was") for all subjects with "be"
- ‚úÖ Correct: "If AI **were** unregulated..."
- ‚ùå Incorrect: "If AI **was** unregulated..."

**AI Examples:**
- "If we **had** more training data, the model **would be** more accurate."
- "If autonomous cars **replaced** human drivers, accidents **would decrease**."
- "What **would happen** if AI **were** completely unregulated?"

---

#### Conditional Type 3 (Hypothetical Past)

**Form:** If + past perfect, would/could/might + have + past participle

**Use:** Imaginary situations in the past (what didn't happen)

**AI Examples:**
- "If we **had used** a larger dataset, the accuracy **would have been** higher."
- "The project **would have succeeded** if we **had chosen** a different algorithm."
- "If they **hadn't implemented** AI, customer satisfaction **wouldn't have improved**."

**Common Errors to Avoid:**
- ‚ùå "If we would have..." ‚Üí ‚úÖ "If we had..."
- ‚ùå "If they had chose..." ‚Üí ‚úÖ "If they had chosen..."
